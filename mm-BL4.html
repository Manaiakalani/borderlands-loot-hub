How to use Golden Keys

You can use Borderlands 4 Golden Keys on one of the in-game Golden Chests. These Golden Chests are located in each faction’s main hub town and show up on the world map and compass.

Opening the Golden Chests costs 1 Golden Key and offers high-quality loot, often these are Epic Items; however, there is also a small chance that the chest contains a Legendary Item.

When should you use Golden Keys?

In my opinion, you can best use your Golden Keys when leveling up through the game because the Golden Chest provides high-quality loot that helps you on your quest. You could save them for the time you reach Max Level; however, by that time, you will be shifting your focus to farming dedicated loot sources for specific legendary gear to optimize your Build.

Borderlands 4 SHiFT Code FAQ

Do you want to know how to use SHiFT Codes, or are you running into any issues, then check out the frequently asked questions below.

What are Borderlands 4 SHiFT Codes?

SHiFT Codes are unique codes that you can redeem in-game or on the SHiFT Website for various items. These can be weapons or cosmetic items, but one of the most popular items has always been the Golden Keys. The Borderlands 4 SHiFT Codes are universal and will work on all platforms.

What are Golden Keys?

Golden Keys are yellow skeleton keys that will let you open up the Golden Skeleton Chests in the faction towns in Borderlands 4.

The Golden Chest offers high-quality loot, often these are Epic Items; however, there is also a small chance that the chest contains a Legendary Item.

How to get Golden Keys for Borderlands 4?

Golden Keys can only be obtained by redeeming SHiFT Codes. The Borderlands channel, Gearbox Software, or Randy Pitchford randomly provides these on their socials or through the official newsletter. You can follow those and hope the Algorithm doesn’t bury the post in your feed, or you can bookmark this page, as I’ll be tracking and posting every SHiFT Code here.

How to redeem SHiFT Codes in Borderlands 4?

There are two ways you can redeem the Borderlands 4 SHiFT Codes:

In-Game – The SHiFT Menu is accessible in the main menu or by pausing the game. Select the Rewards Tab and enter the SHiFT Code in the Code Redemption field.

SHiFT Website – On the Gearbox SHiFT Site, you can use the ‘Code Redemption’ feature to claim your rewards.

What if a SHiFT Code isn’t working?

SHiFT Codes can expire; however, when they haven’t expired yet, sometimes things don’t work as intended. It is possible that the SHiFT service is having hiccups or that the SHiFT code is buggy.

Try redeeming the code again. In the past, there were instances where a code wouldn’t redeem on the site but did in-game. There have also been reports that a single code wouldn’t redeem on someone’s account, but others worked just fine.

If you keep having issues, there might be a solution to your problem in the knowledge base, or you can submit a support ticket with 2K Games. Try to describe the issue as best as possible, as this will help them better understand and resolve the issue.
